Interior Design

At Hanova Cabinets, we understand the importance of evolving your home to suit your growing family and changing lifestyle needs. Whether you’re looking to downsize or expand your living space, our Creative Interior Design team is here to transform your home into the space of your dreams.

We have partnered with Western Australia’s finest suppliers and skilled craftsmen to deliver cost-effective, well-planned renovations, ensuring a smoother process for you from start to finish.

Our Interior Design Team offers a comprehensive design service that turns your ideas into reality. From space planning and material selection to project management, we provide detailed plans, 3D drawings, and full documentation to help you make informed decisions with confidence.

Renovations

Hanova Cabinets is a family-owned business based in Perth, Western Australia, with over 5 years of experience in the industry. The company has undertaken numerous projects across the residential, commercial, and renovation markets. With a history rooted in quality craftsmanship and design, Hanova Cabinets has the flexibility to produce and design cabinetry work ranging from mid to high-end.

The company's extensive building knowledge ensures that it is recognized as a reliable and well-equipped builder. As a registered unrestricted builder, Hanova Cabinets offers a wide range of services, from refurbishing and renovating to new builds. The company takes full control of each project, ensuring that clients are fully informed and updated throughout the entire process.

By getting to know each client, Hanova Cabinets aims to build not just their dream project but also a lasting relationship, providing excellent service and valuable knowledge for future reference. The company strives to maintain long-term relationships with clients, encouraging them to return and refer others. Our directors are fully involved in the operations, ensuring the best services and highest quality products at all times.
